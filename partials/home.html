Editorial Photography

Editorial photography offers great carrer opportunities for photographers. First, if your work is chosen for the cover of the publication your audience and market exposure will grow exponentially. Another opportunity is the possibility of expanding your clientele and portfolio, as the assignment could lead to other potential offers by the client and you could sell your photographs as stock photography.

Fruits in Acrylics

Finding subjects to photograph is a challenging task for any photographer. Some photographers prefer people as subjects fpr portraiture or street photography, others choose the landscape and the natural would, and some like me prefer abstract subjects. In this example of Fruits in Acrylics, the subject was completely covered in acrylics using the pouring technique to create the pattern. The subject was photographed as a whole and then as sections studying perspective, shape and form.

Creating a Series for exhibition

The word exhibition could be nerve racking for any artist, as it takes a tremendous amout of work getting the artwork ready, marketing and setting it up the exhibit space. However, once you overcome that fear you find that exhibiting your work is a thrilling and rewarding experience. Not only will you grow as an artist by receiving feedback from visitors and art critiques but by exposing yourself to new challenges.
